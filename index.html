WEB TYPOGRAPHIC POSTER

Inspiration

I wanted to create a minimalistic poster that would reflect my typeface, so I incorporated simple geometric motifs such as lines and circles to use within my poster.

I wanted to create some minimalistic, but could still be a display typeface; at first I was less concerned about its readability, and through further iteration, my typeface's aesthetic slightly shifted.

Another Section Title

At first, yellow motifs would help to form the shape of each letterform, and in combination with a few limited single lines, they would create the letters.

The yellow motifs ended up being too distracting, and I focused on forming the letters with just the singly weight lines. I also shifted the stems of the letters to make them cleaner.

At this point, I re-incorporated the colour into the typeface to see how it could contribute to the form of the letters, and could be used in every glyph.

However, I decided on only having the single line weight letters, and made them cleaner and minimalistic.
